Padel in Konyaaltı, Antalya

Konyaaltı is the most convenient area for padel on the western, coastal side of Antalya. Here is where to play.

Konyaaltı sits west of the city centre, along Konyaaltı beach and the Liman / Hurma neighbourhoods. If you are staying on this side of Antalya, it is the handiest area for padel and avoids cross-city traffic.

Covered courts matter here: Antalya summers are hot, and indoor courts let you play comfortably at midday and into the evening, as well as during the occasional winter rain.

Getting on court without a partner

You do not need to bring a full four. Community clubs in Konyaaltı run open games and mixers matched by level — message the club and ask to join a session.

FAQ

Where can I play padel in Konyaaltı?

The main dedicated club is V7 Padel Antalya at Hürriyet Caddesi No: 51, with 4 covered indoor courts, coaching and open games.

Is there indoor padel in Konyaaltı?

Yes — V7 Padel Antalya has 4 covered courts, so you can play through summer heat and winter rain.

How do I book a court in Konyaaltı?

Book through the Matchpoint app or message the club on WhatsApp at +90 546 780 0877.
