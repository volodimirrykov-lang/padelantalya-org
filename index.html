Where to play padel in Antalya

Padel has grown fast in Antalya over the last few years. Courts are concentrated in three areas: Konyaaltı (west, near the coast), Muratpaşa / Lara (city centre and east) and the Belek resort belt. Below are the main dedicated clubs.

How much does it cost?

As a rough guide, court rental in Antalya runs from about 1,000–1,800 TL per hour per court, depending on the club, the time of day and the season. Indoor/premium courts sit at the upper end. Coaching (private or group) and membership packages are priced separately — ask the club directly for current rates.

Playing without a partner

You don't need to bring three friends. Community clubs run open games and mixers where solo players are matched into a game by level. V7 Padel, for example, organises club games and matchmaking almost daily — message the club through its app or WhatsApp and ask to join an open session.

Coaching & tournaments

Most dedicated clubs offer lessons from beginner (grip, court movement, basic strokes) through to advanced training camps with visiting high-level coaches. Tournaments, leagues and club formats run throughout the season — a good way to meet other players and improve quickly.

Frequently asked questions

Where can I play padel in Antalya?

Mainly in Konyaaltı, Muratpaşa/Lara and the Belek resort belt. The main dedicated clubs are V7 Padel Antalya (Konyaaltı, 4 covered courts), Lara Padel Club and Mediterra Padel.

How much is a court?

Roughly 1,000–1,800 TL per hour per court, depending on club, time and season.

Can I play without a partner?

Yes — community clubs such as V7 Padel run open games and mixers for solo players. Book through the club's app or WhatsApp.

Is there indoor padel?

Yes. V7 Padel Antalya in Konyaaltı has 4 covered premium courts, playable year-round.
